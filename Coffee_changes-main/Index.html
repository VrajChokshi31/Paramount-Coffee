About Us

At Paramount Original Coffee, we source our coffee from the original birthplace of coffee – Ethiopia!

Ethiopia is world-famous for its coffee. Honey washed Dawi, Organic Bekama Solinté, Wild Sidamo, Yirgacheffe, Oromia Gomma Microlot and Organic Sidamo are six of the best coffee varieties in the world.

Our goal is to provide these exotic flavours at the best possible prices. We have great relationships with all our farmers, paying them more than fair trade standards. Get in touch with Paramount Original Coffee specialists today to find out how you can taste our exotic flavours.
